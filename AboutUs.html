Welcome to our Website

This website was created as part of a Community Engagement Program for the Rural Tech Initiative course in the second year of the BCA program at Pratap College Amalner (Autonomous). The project focuses on raising awareness about traffic rules in rural areas and was developed by the students of the Computer Applications department.
